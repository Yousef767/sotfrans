Our Translators

The most important factor for success of any translation service provider is the human capital, i.e. the translators. Knowing this, we started our business by creating a professional in-house translation team to provide our customers with the highest quality translation services. All our translators are highly qualified, with a long-term experience in mostly all translation fields. Also, Softrans prides its professional network of experienced freelancers around the world. We only use native speakers of target language to make sure they are well acquainted with the culture of the audience they are targeting in addition to full awareness of all nuances and subtleties of the target language.

Translator Selection Criteria

To ascertain our translation services are the best ever in the market and satisfy our quality-concerned clients, we apply a strict set of criteria in selecting our translators to ensure they have the necessary qualifications, experience and know-how. Softrans adopts a precise filtering process in selecting our in-house translators. The same and even more rigorous process is applied when adding any freelancer to our database.

Our translator selection criteria include

Recognized academic degree in translation and linguistic studies
At least 5 years of experience in a translation and localization environment.
Extensive knowledge in the translation fields we handle
Deep technical know-how in CAT tools and translation techniques
Specialization in at least 3 translation fields plus mastering general translation
Professional attitude and commitment to customer instructions and deadlines.
Ability to work under pressure and translate large volumes in reasonable time
